```
Taply transforms how professionals network. Replace paper business cards with smart digital cards that you can share instantly via QR code, NFC tag, or messaging apps.

── WHY TAPLY? ──

• Go paperless — create unlimited digital business cards
• Share instantly with QR codes people can scan
• Tap phones together with NFC for contactless exchange
• Track who viewed your card with built-in analytics
• Organize contacts by categories and favorites
• Export as high-resolution PNG or VCF contact file
• Works in English and Arabic

── CORE FEATURES ──

DIGITAL BUSINESS CARDS
Design your perfect card with 17 customizable fields — name, title, company, phone, email, website, social links, and more. Choose from multiple professionally designed templates. Edit anytime. No printing costs.

QR CODE SHARING
Every card generates a unique QR code. Others scan it with any phone camera and instantly save your details. Save your QR code to your gallery or share it anywhere.

NFC TAP-TO-SHARE
Program NFC tags with your card data. A simple tap shares your full profile. Read others NFC tags to save their cards directly to your contact list.

CATEGORIES & FAVORITES
Organize your network with custom color-coded categories. Mark important contacts as favorites. Filter and sort by category, date, name, or favorites.

ANALYTICS DASHBOARD
See how your network grows. Track total QR scans, NFC interactions, profile views, shares, VCF downloads, and contact saves with daily, weekly, and monthly breakdowns.

NETWORKING SCORE
Get a personalized networking score out of 100. The algorithm analyzes your activity across 7 weighted dimensions — diversity, frequency, reach, responsiveness, organization, growth, and engagement — and provides actionable tips to improve.

CRM NOTES
Attach notes to scanned contacts so you never forget context: where you met, what you discussed, follow-up reminders.

EXPORT & BACKUP
Export any card as a high-resolution PNG image (up to 1920px) or industry-standard VCF file compatible with all smartphone address books. Save directly to your photo gallery.

Download Taply today and make your first impression unforgettable.
```
